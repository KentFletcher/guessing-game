A Little About Me:

My name is Kent Sheats. I moved to Washington in October of 2019 from Pennsylvania. I was born in Florida. I love the outdoors and often hike, snowboard, camp and kayak. I am married.

I graduated from Kutztown University of Pennsylvania in 2003. I have a bachelors degree in Business Administration with a concentration in marketing.

I have spent the last five and a half years doing landscaping and managing a small landscape company, specializing in custom stonework. I also worked for myself doing drywall installation, mainly as a subcontractor. I have also worked in small engine sales and repair.

I hope to continue to travel the world, my three dream destinations are Hawaii, Alaska, and Europe. I would also love to find a career that I can really love, something that challenges me and pushes me to continuously grow.
